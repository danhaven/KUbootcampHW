Summary: Latitude vs. X


The point of this project was to compare factors that impact weather as you get closer to the equator.To do this we pulled weather data on over 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API.

After compiling the data, we used Matplotlib to plot the different factors of the weather vs. latitude. Factors we looked at included temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. This page summarizes our observations from this analysis and shows each of our visualizations created. It also gives further explanations of each.

Observation 1 - There is not a super strong correlation between latitude and humidity, but the greatest amount of occurances are around the equator as expected.
Observation 2 - The highest max temps occur in the cities with latitudes between -20 and 20.
Observation 3 - A majority of the cities in the dataset are in the northern hemisphere. As this is a random sampling of cities of the world, it can be inferred that the majority of world cities are in the northen hemisphere.
